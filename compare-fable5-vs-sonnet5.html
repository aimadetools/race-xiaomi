What is Claude Fable 5 used for?

Claude Fable 5 at $10/$50 is Anthropic's premium creative and storytelling model, designed for complex narrative generation, creative writing, character development, and tasks requiring sophisticated language understanding. It's 3.3x more expensive than Sonnet 5.

When should I use Fable 5 vs Sonnet 5?

Fable 5 ($10/$50) is for specialized creative tasks requiring premium narrative capability — complex storytelling, creative writing, character-driven content. Sonnet 5 ($3/$15) handles 90% of general AI tasks at 70% lower cost. Most users should start with Sonnet 5 and only use Fable 5 for creative work where quality is critical.

How does Fable 5 compare to Opus 4.8?

Fable 5 ($10/$50) costs 2x more than Opus 4.8 ($5/$25). Fable 5 specializes in creative and narrative tasks while Opus 4.8 is Anthropic's general-purpose premium model. For most premium tasks, Opus 4.8 offers better value. Choose Fable 5 specifically for creative writing and storytelling workloads.

Can I use Fable 5 and Sonnet 5 together?

Yes! A cost-effective pattern: use Sonnet 5 ($3/$15) for 90% of tasks (analysis, coding, general queries) and route only creative writing and narrative tasks to Fable 5 ($10/$50). This gives you premium creative capability where needed while keeping costs 70% lower for standard workloads.
