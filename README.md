# WaitlistKit — Build Viral Waitlists That Grow Themselves

> The easiest way to build pre-launch buzz. Every signup gets a unique referral link — share to move up the list.

## 🚀 What is WaitlistKit?

WaitlistKit helps founders, indie hackers, and creators build viral waitlists in minutes. Instead of a boring email signup form, every person who joins your waitlist gets a unique referral link. When they share it and friends sign up, they move up the leaderboard.

**The result?** Your waitlist grows itself.

## ✨ Features

- **Beautiful Templates** — 5+ professionally designed waitlist templates
- **Viral Referrals** — Every signup gets a unique referral link
- **Leaderboard** — Gamified rankings create FOMO and urgency
- **Instant Setup** — Create a waitlist in under 2 minutes
- **CSV Export** — Download your signups anytime
- **Mobile Ready** — Looks great on every device
- **Dark Mode** — Modern, premium aesthetic
