Frequently Asked Questions

Is Llama 4 Maverick cheaper than Claude 4 Sonnet?

Yes, dramatically. Claude 4 Sonnet costs $3/M input and $15/M output. Llama 4 Maverick costs $0.27/M input and $0.85/M output. Llama 4 Maverick is 91% cheaper on input and 94% cheaper on output. For a typical workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, Llama 4 Maverick costs $0.695 vs Claude 4 Sonnet's $10.50 — saving you $9.80/month (93%).

How does Llama 4 Maverick quality compare to Claude 4 Sonnet?

Claude 4 Sonnet was Anthropic's balanced mid-tier model with strong reasoning and coding, but it was retired on June 15, 2026. Llama 4 Maverick from Meta (available via Together.ai) is an open-source model with 1M context (5x more than Sonnet 4's 200K). While Claude 4 Sonnet may have edges in certain reasoning tasks, Llama 4 Maverick offers competitive quality for most standard workloads at 91% lower cost. For a direct Anthropic successor, Claude Sonnet 4.6 ($3/$15) has the same pricing but adds 1M context.

What happened when Claude 4 Sonnet was retired on June 15?

Since June 15, 2026, Claude 4 Sonnet API calls return 410 Gone errors. You must migrate to a replacement model. Options: (1) Claude Sonnet 4.6 ($3/$15) — direct Anthropic successor, same price, 5x more context. (2) Llama 4 Maverick ($0.27/$0.85) — 91% cheaper, open source, 1M context. (3) DeepSeek V4 Pro ($0.44/$0.87) — budget open source, 1M context. (4) Gemini 2.5 Pro ($1.25/$10) — Google, 1M context.

Is Llama 4 Maverick open source?

Yes. Llama 4 Maverick is released by Meta under the MIT license, one of the most permissive open-source licenses. This means you can use it commercially, modify it, and self-host it without any licensing fees. The API pricing through Together.ai ($0.27/$0.85 per 1M tokens) is for hosted access — you can also self-host for even lower cost if you have the infrastructure.
