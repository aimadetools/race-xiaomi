Best Free Tier by Use Case

Not sure which provider to start with? Match your project to the best free option.

Chatbot / Conversational AI

Need low latency and high daily volume. Google's free tier handles 21K+ requests/day with no cost.

Pick: Google Gemini (unlimited)

RAG / Document Q&A

Long context windows matter. Gemini 2.5 Pro offers 1M context free. Cohere excels at retrieval.

Pick: Google Gemini or Cohere

Code Generation

Quality matters most. Test Claude Sonnet 4 and GPT-4o with $5 credits, then pick the better fit.

Pick: Anthropic + OpenAI (test both)

Content Generation

High volume, moderate quality. Google's unlimited free tier is the only viable long-term option.

Pick: Google Gemini (unlimited)

Data Classification

Short inputs, short outputs, high volume. Budget models on Google's free tier work perfectly.

Pick: Google Gemini Flash (free)

Learning / Education

Experimenting with different models. Use all $5 credits to compare quality, then settle on Google.

Pick: All providers (use all credits)

Google is the only viable long-term free option

Every other provider's $5 credit runs out within days of real usage. If you're building something you want to run for free for more than a week, Google Gemini is your only choice. The 15 RPM limit is enough for most side projects.

Use $5 credits for quality testing, not production

The smart strategy: use OpenAI, Anthropic, and Mistral credits to test which model produces the best output for your use case. Then commit to one paid provider (or use Google free tier) for production.

Budget models stretch credits 10-50x further

Using GPT-4o mini instead of GPT-4o makes your $5 credit last 10x longer. Using Claude Haiku instead of Opus stretches it 50x. Always start with budget models during evaluation.
