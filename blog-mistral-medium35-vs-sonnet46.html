The European Advantage

Mistral's biggest differentiator isn't just price — it's data sovereignty. Headquartered in Paris with European data storage, Mistral simplifies GDPR compliance for EU companies.

  • GDPR compliance — Data stays in EU servers
  • No US data transfer — Avoids Schrems II complications
  • EU-based company — Subject to EU regulations, not US CLOUD Act
  • Open-weight models — Self-host in your own EU infrastructure

Claude's Quality Edge

Claude Sonnet 4.6 isn't just more expensive for no reason. It has real advantages:

  • 8x larger context — 1M vs 128K tokens for long documents
  • Best-in-class coding — Widely regarded as the top coding assistant
  • Safety focus — Anthropic's Constitutional AI approach
  • Larger ecosystem — More integrations, tools, and community support

The Verdict

If you're an EU company needing GDPR compliance, Mistral Medium 3.5 is a no-brainer — 50% cheaper with built-in data sovereignty. If you need the absolute best coding quality or 1M context, Claude Sonnet 4.6 justifies the premium.

At $1,080/year in savings, Mistral's price advantage adds up fast. The real question is whether Claude's quality difference matters for your specific workload.
