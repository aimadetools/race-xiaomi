Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mistral Medium 3.5 cheaper than Claude 4 Sonnet?

Yes. Claude 4 Sonnet costs $3/M input and $15/M output. Mistral Medium 3.5 costs $1.50/M input and $7.50/M output. Mistral Medium 3.5 is exactly 50% cheaper on both input and output. For a typical workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, Mistral Medium 3.5 costs $5.25 vs Claude 4 Sonnet's $10.50 — saving you $5.25/month (50%).

How does Mistral Medium 3.5 quality compare to Claude 4 Sonnet?

Claude 4 Sonnet was Anthropic's balanced mid-tier model with strong reasoning and coding, but it was retired on June 15, 2026. Mistral Medium 3.5 from Mistral AI (France) offers competitive quality at 50% lower cost with EU data sovereignty. For a direct Anthropic successor, Claude Sonnet 4.6 ($3/$15) has the same pricing but adds 1M context (5x more than Sonnet 4's 200K). Mistral Medium 3.5 is ideal for EU-focused or budget-conscious applications.

What happened when Claude 4 Sonnet was retired on June 15?

Since June 15, 2026, Claude 4 Sonnet API calls return 410 Gone errors. You must migrate to a replacement model. Options: (1) Claude Sonnet 4.6 ($3/$15) — direct Anthropic successor, same price, 5x more context. (2) Mistral Medium 3.5 ($1.50/$7.50) — 50% cheaper, EU data sovereignty. (3) Gemini 2.5 Pro ($1.25/$10) — Google, 1M context. (4) DeepSeek V4 Pro ($0.44/$0.87) — budget open source, 1M context.

Does Mistral Medium 3.5 support EU data sovereignty?

Yes. Mistral AI is a French company headquartered in Paris. All Mistral Medium 3.5 API processing occurs within EU data centers, making it fully compliant with GDPR and EU data sovereignty requirements. This is a key advantage over US-based providers like Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google for European companies with strict data residency requirements.
