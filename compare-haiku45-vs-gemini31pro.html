Frequently Asked Questions

Is Claude Haiku 4.5 cheaper than Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Yes. Claude Haiku 4.5 costs $1/$5 per 1M tokens. Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $2/$12 per 1M tokens. Haiku is 50% cheaper on input and 58% cheaper on output — a significant difference for cost-sensitive workloads.

Which has a larger context window?

Gemini 3.1 Pro has a 1M token context window, while Claude Haiku 4.5 has a 200K token context window. Gemini offers 5x the context — critical if you process very long documents or codebases.

When should I choose Claude Haiku 4.5 over Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Choose Claude Haiku 4.5 when cost is the primary concern and your inputs fit within 200K tokens. It's 50-58% cheaper across the board. Choose Gemini 3.1 Pro when you need the 1M context window or stronger reasoning capabilities.

How do these compare to DeepSeek V4 Pro?

DeepSeek V4 Pro costs $0.435/$0.87 per 1M tokens — cheaper than both with 1M context. However, Claude Haiku 4.5 and Gemini 3.1 Pro offer stronger reasoning and better instruction-following. For pure cost optimization, DeepSeek V4 Pro wins.
