Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mistral Small 4 cheaper than Claude Opus 4.8?

Yes, dramatically. Mistral Small 4 costs $0.10/$0.30 per 1M tokens while Opus 4.8 costs $5.00/$25.00. That's 98% cheaper on input and 99% cheaper on output. For high-volume workloads, Mistral Small 4 can be 80x cheaper overall.

Which has a larger context window?

Claude Opus 4.8 has a 1M token context window, 7.8x larger than Mistral Small 4's 128K context window. This makes Opus 4.8 far better for processing long documents, complex coding tasks, and analysis that benefits from extensive context.

When should I choose Claude Opus 4.8 over Mistral Small 4?

Choose Claude Opus 4.8 when you need complex coding, deep analysis, or the full 1M context window. Opus 4.8 excels at multi-step reasoning, code generation, and tasks requiring nuanced understanding. It's the premium choice for developer workflows.

When should I choose Mistral Small 4 over Claude Opus 4.8?

Choose Mistral Small 4 when cost is your primary concern and your tasks are relatively straightforward. Mistral Small 4 is ideal for high-volume classification, simple content generation, data extraction, and batch processing at $0.10/$0.30 per 1M tokens.

How much can I save switching from Claude Opus 4.8 to Mistral Small 4?
