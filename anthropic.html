Which Claude Model Should You Use?

Choose the right model based on your workload, quality requirements, and budget.

Complex Reasoning & Analysis

Multi-step reasoning, research synthesis, legal analysis, and tasks requiring the highest accuracy. Best-in-class performance on graduate-level benchmarks.

Recommended: Claude 4 Opus

Code Generation & Agentic Tasks

Writing, debugging, and reviewing code. Building multi-step workflows and tool-use agents. Strong balance of capability and speed.

Recommended: Claude Sonnet 4

High-Volume Chat & Classification

Customer support chatbots, content moderation, sentiment analysis, and classification tasks. Excellent quality at a fraction of the cost.

Recommended: Claude Haiku 3.5

Content Generation at Scale

Summarization, translation, and content creation where throughput matters. 200K context handles large documents with ease.

Recommended: Claude Sonnet 4 or Haiku 3.5

Enterprise Document Processing

Analyzing lengthy contracts, research papers, or codebases. The 200K context window processes entire documents in a single call.

Recommended: Claude Sonnet 4

Startup MVPs & Prototyping

Rapid prototyping and MVP development where cost efficiency is critical. Start with Haiku for most tasks, upgrade to Sonnet when needed.

Recommended: Claude Haiku 3.5
