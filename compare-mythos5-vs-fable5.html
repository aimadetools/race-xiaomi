Frequently Asked Questions

Is Claude Mythos 5 or Claude Fable 5 cheaper?

Both models cost exactly the same: $10.00 input and $50.00 output per 1M tokens. The choice comes down to capabilities, not price. Both have 1M token context windows.

What's the difference between Claude Mythos 5 and Claude Fable 5?

Both are Anthropic premium models at $10/$50 per 1M tokens with 1M context windows. Mythos 5 focuses on creative writing, narrative reasoning, and nuanced analysis. Fable 5 excels at structured data processing, technical writing, and systematic problem-solving.

Which is better for coding: Claude Mythos 5 or Fable 5?

Claude Fable 5 is generally better for coding and technical tasks. Its structured reasoning approach handles complex code generation, debugging, and architecture decisions well. Mythos 5 can code but its strength is creative and narrative tasks.

Should I upgrade from Opus 4.8 to Mythos 5 or Fable 5?

Both Mythos 5 and Fable 5 cost $10/$50 vs Opus 4.8 at $5/$25 — double the price. Upgrade if you need specific capabilities that match their strengths. For most users, Opus 4.8 at half the price is sufficient.

Can I use both Mythos 5 and Fable 5 together?

Yes! Many teams route creative tasks (marketing, content) to Mythos 5 and technical tasks (code, data) to Fable 5. Since they cost the same, you get the best of both worlds without budget complexity.
