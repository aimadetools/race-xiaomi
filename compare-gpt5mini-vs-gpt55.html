Frequently Asked Questions

Is GPT-5 mini cheaper than GPT-5.5?

Yes — GPT-5 mini is dramatically cheaper. GPT-5 mini costs $0.25/M input and $2/M output, while GPT-5.5 costs $5/M input and $30/M output. That's 95% cheaper on input and 93% cheaper on output.

Which is better for chatbots — GPT-5 mini or GPT-5.5?

For most chatbot use cases, GPT-5 mini is the better choice due to its 93-95% lower cost. GPT-5.5 makes sense when you need premium output quality or the larger 1.05M context window for complex conversations.

How much would I save using GPT-5 mini instead of GPT-5.5?

For a workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, GPT-5 mini costs $1.25/month vs GPT-5.5's $20/month — saving $18.75/month (94%). At scale (10M input + 5M output), you save $187.50/month — $2,250/year.

When should I choose GPT-5.5 over GPT-5 mini?

Choose GPT-5.5 when: (1) you need premium output quality for complex tasks, (2) you need the larger 1.05M context window (vs GPT-5 mini's 272K), (3) cost is not a primary concern and quality is paramount. For everything else, GPT-5 mini delivers excellent value at 93-95% lower cost.
