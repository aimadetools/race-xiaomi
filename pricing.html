Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really start for free?

Yes! The free tier is genuinely useful. You can create a waitlist, collect up to 100 signups, and use referral tracking. No credit card required.

What happens when I hit 100 signups on the free plan?

Your waitlist still works — people can still sign up. But you'll need to upgrade to see new signups beyond 100 and access premium features.

Is the $29 Launch Package really one-time?

Yes! Pay once, use it forever. No recurring charges. We believe in fair pricing for indie hackers.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. With the Launch Package or Pro plan, you can export your entire waitlist as a CSV file anytime. Your data is yours.

How does referral tracking work?

Every signup gets a unique referral link. When someone signs up through that link, the referrer gets credit. We track this automatically and display a leaderboard.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime. No contracts, no questions asked. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
