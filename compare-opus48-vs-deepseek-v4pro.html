Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is DeepSeek V4 Pro than Claude Opus 4.8?

DeepSeek V4 Pro costs $0.435/$0.87 per 1M tokens while Claude Opus 4.8 costs $5/$25 per 1M tokens. DeepSeek is 91% cheaper on input and 97% cheaper on output. For a typical workload, DeepSeek costs $1.70/mo vs Opus 4.8's $50/mo — a 97% savings.

Is DeepSeek V4 Pro as good as Claude Opus 4.8?

Claude Opus 4.8 is Anthropic's most powerful model, designed for maximum reasoning quality. DeepSeek V4 Pro is a budget model optimized for cost-efficiency. For most common tasks (chatbots, content generation, data extraction), DeepSeek delivers excellent results at 91-97% lower cost. Opus 4.8 excels at complex multi-step reasoning and tasks requiring the highest quality output.

When should I choose Claude Opus 4.8 over DeepSeek V4 Pro?

Choose Claude Opus 4.8 when you need: (1) Maximum reasoning quality for complex tasks, (2) Enterprise-grade reliability from Anthropic, (3) Tasks where output quality directly impacts revenue or reputation. Choose DeepSeek V4 Pro for high-volume, cost-sensitive workloads where excellent-enough quality at 91% lower cost is the priority.

Can I mix Claude Opus 4.8 and DeepSeek V4 Pro?

Yes. Route simple, high-volume tasks (chatbots, content generation, data extraction) to DeepSeek V4 Pro at $0.435/$0.87, and reserve Claude Opus 4.8 ($5/$25) for complex reasoning and tasks requiring maximum quality. This tiered strategy can reduce costs by 80-90% while maintaining quality where it matters most.
