Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is DeepSeek V4 Flash than Sonnet 4.6?

DeepSeek V4 Flash is 95% cheaper on input ($0.14 vs $3.00 per 1M tokens) and 98% cheaper on output ($0.28 vs $15.00 per 1M tokens). For a typical workload of 1M input and 500K output tokens per month, DeepSeek V4 Flash costs $0.28 vs Sonnet 4.6's $10.50 — a savings of $10.22 per month.

Is DeepSeek V4 Flash as good as Sonnet 4.6?

Sonnet 4.6 is Anthropic's mid-tier model, designed for versatile, high-quality performance across a wide range of tasks. DeepSeek V4 Flash is DeepSeek's ultra-budget model optimized for cost-efficiency. For most common tasks (chatbots, content generation, data extraction), DeepSeek V4 Flash delivers solid results at 95-98% lower cost. Sonnet 4.6 excels at complex reasoning, nuanced writing, and tasks requiring higher quality output.

When should I choose Sonnet 4.6 over DeepSeek V4 Flash?

Choose Sonnet 4.6 when you need: (1) Higher quality for complex reasoning and code review tasks, (2) Anthropic's safety and alignment guarantees, (3) Better performance on nuanced, multi-step tasks. Choose DeepSeek V4 Flash when cost efficiency matters most — it's 95-98% cheaper with solid performance for most workloads, and both share the same 1M token context window.

Do both models have the same context window?

Yes, both Claude Sonnet 4.6 and DeepSeek V4 Flash support a 1M token context window. This means you get the same ability to process large documents, long conversations, and extensive codebases regardless of which model you choose. DeepSeek V4 Flash delivers this same context capacity at a fraction of the cost.
