How to Forecast AI API Costs

Forecasting AI API costs requires three inputs: cost per request, current volume, and growth rate. Most startups underestimate growth — a 20% monthly increase means your costs triple in 6 months.

The Cost Projection Formula

Monthly cost = (requests/day × 30) × (input_tokens × input_price + output_tokens × output_price) / 1,000,000

With growth: Month N cost = Month 1 cost × (1 + growth_rate)^(N-1)

Typical Growth Rates by Stage

  • MVP / Pre-launch: 30-50% monthly — rapid user acquisition
  • Early traction: 15-30% monthly — organic growth + word of mouth
  • Growth stage: 10-20% monthly — scaling with marketing
  • Established product: 5-10% monthly — incremental feature usage
  • Enterprise: 2-5% monthly — existing customer usage growth

When to Optimize Your AI Costs

  • At $100/month: Start tracking costs per feature — you need visibility before it grows
  • At $500/month: Compare budget models — GPT-4o mini or Gemini Flash can replace premium models for 80% of tasks
  • At $1,000/month: Implement model routing — use Haiku/Flash for simple tasks, premium for complex ones
  • At $5,000/month: Negotiate volume discounts or explore fine-tuning for high-volume patterns
  • At $10,000+/month: Consider dedicated inference (Together.ai, Fireworks) or self-hosting open-source models
