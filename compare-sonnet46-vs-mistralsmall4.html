Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is Mistral Small 4 than Sonnet 4.6?

Mistral Small 4 is 97% cheaper on input ($0.10 vs $3.00 per 1M tokens) and 98% cheaper on output ($0.30 vs $15.00 per 1M tokens). For a typical workload of 1M input and 500K output tokens per month, Mistral Small 4 costs $0.25 vs Sonnet 4.6's $10.50 — a savings of $10.25 per month.

Is Mistral Small 4 as good as Sonnet 4.6?

Sonnet 4.6 is Anthropic's mid-tier model designed for high-quality reasoning and versatile tasks. Mistral Small 4 is Mistral's budget model optimized for cost-efficiency. For most common tasks (chatbots, content generation, data extraction), Mistral Small 4 delivers solid results at 97% lower cost. Sonnet 4.6 excels at complex reasoning, nuanced analysis, and tasks requiring a larger context window (1M vs 128K tokens).

When should I choose Sonnet 4.6 over Mistral Small 4?

Choose Sonnet 4.6 when you need: (1) Higher quality reasoning for complex tasks, (2) A much larger context window (1M vs 128K tokens), (3) Better performance on nuanced, multi-step analysis. Choose Mistral Small 4 when cost efficiency is the priority — it's 97% cheaper with good enough performance for most high-volume workloads.

Does the smaller context window (128K vs 1M) matter?

It depends on your use case. For chatbots, content generation, and most API calls, 128K tokens is more than sufficient. However, if you need to process very long documents, large codebases, or maintain extensive conversation histories in a single call, Sonnet 4.6's 1M context window (7.8x larger) becomes a significant advantage. Most users find 128K adequate for their needs.
