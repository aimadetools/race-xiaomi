The Verdict

GPT-5.6 Luna wins on price across the board.

GPT-5.6 Luna ($1/$6) is 33% cheaper than Gemini 3.5 Flash ($1.50/$9) on both input and output. Both offer 1M+ context windows. For most budget-conscious workloads, Luna delivers better value. Gemini 3.5 Flash may have advantages for Google-specific tasks or if you prefer Google's ecosystem.

When to Choose GPT-5.6 Luna

  • Cost-first workloads: At $1/$6, Luna is the cheapest 1M+ context model from OpenAI. 33% less than Gemini 3.5 Flash across the board.
  • High-volume APIs: For chatbots, content generation, or translation at scale, Luna's 33% price advantage compounds fast.
  • OpenAI ecosystem: If you're already using OpenAI's API and want the cheapest 1M+ context option.

When to Choose Gemini 3.5 Flash

  • Google ecosystem: If you're already using Google Cloud and want to stay in the GCP ecosystem.
  • Google-specific tasks: Gemini may have advantages for tasks involving Google services, multilingual content, or specific training data.
  • Established track record: Gemini Flash models have been in production longer and may have more battle-tested reliability.
