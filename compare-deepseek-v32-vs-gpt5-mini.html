Frequently Asked Questions

Is DeepSeek V3.2 cheaper than GPT-5 Mini?

Yes, on both input and output. DeepSeek V3.2 costs $0.23/M input (8% cheaper) and $0.34/M output (83% cheaper). For output-heavy workloads, DeepSeek V3.2 offers massive savings.

Which has a larger context window?

GPT-5 Mini has a 272K token context window — 2.1x larger than DeepSeek V3.2's 128K. For long documents or extended conversations, GPT-5 Mini has a significant advantage.

When should I choose GPT-5 Mini?

Choose GPT-5 Mini when you need more than 128K context (large documents, long conversations) or prefer OpenAI's ecosystem and API compatibility.

Are budget models good enough for production?

Both are production-ready for many use cases: chatbots, content generation, data extraction, classification, and summarization. For complex reasoning or high-stakes decisions, consider mid-tier models like GPT-5 ($1.25/$10) or Claude Sonnet 4 ($3/$15).
