Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is GPT-5 Mini than Grok 4.3?

GPT-5 Mini costs $0.25/$2.00 per 1M tokens. Grok 4.3 costs $1.25/$2.50 per 1M tokens. GPT-5 Mini is 80% cheaper on input and 20% cheaper on output — a 50% overall savings for most workloads.

Which has a larger context window?

Grok 4.3 has a 1M token context window, while GPT-5 Mini has a 272K token context window. Grok offers 3.7x the context — important if you process long documents or need extensive conversation history.

When should I choose GPT-5 Mini over Grok 4.3?

Choose GPT-5 Mini when cost is your primary concern — it's 80% cheaper on input tokens, making it ideal for high-volume, input-heavy workloads. Choose Grok 4.3 when you need a larger context window or mid-tier reasoning quality.

Is GPT-5 Mini good enough to replace Grok 4.3?

For many budget-conscious use cases — yes. GPT-5 Mini handles chatbots, content generation, and code assistance at 50-80% lower cost. However, Grok 4.3 may outperform on complex reasoning and offers a much larger context window.
