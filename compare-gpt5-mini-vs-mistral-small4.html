Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mistral Small 4 cheaper than GPT-5 mini?

Yes. Mistral Small 4 costs $0.10/M input (60% cheaper than GPT-5 mini's $0.25) and $0.30/M output (85% cheaper than GPT-5 mini's $2.00). For output-heavy workloads, Mistral Small 4 is roughly 7x cheaper.

How much would I save switching from GPT-5 mini to Mistral Small 4?

For a workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, GPT-5 mini costs $1.25 vs Mistral Small 4's $0.25 — saving $1.00/month (80%). At scale (10M input + 5M output), you save $10/month — $120/year.

What's the quality difference between GPT-5 mini and Mistral Small 4?

GPT-5 mini offers OpenAI's quality at a budget price with a larger 272K context window. Mistral Small 4 is optimized for speed and cost with a 128K context window. Both handle chat, summarization, and basic coding well. GPT-5 mini has the edge on complex reasoning; Mistral Small 4 wins on raw cost efficiency.

When should I choose GPT-5 mini over Mistral Small 4?

Choose GPT-5 mini when: (1) you need a larger context window (272K vs 128K), (2) you prefer OpenAI's ecosystem and tooling, (3) you need slightly better reasoning quality. Choose Mistral Small 4 when: (1) cost is the top priority, (2) 128K context is sufficient, (3) you want 60-85% savings on every request.
