Frequently Asked Questions

Is GPT-5 Mini cheaper than GPT-5?

Yes, significantly. GPT-5 Mini costs $0.25/M input and $2.00/M output. GPT-5 costs $1.25/M input and $10.00/M output. GPT-5 Mini is 80% cheaper on both input and output. For a typical workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, GPT-5 Mini costs $1.25 vs GPT-5's $6.25 — saving $5.00/month (80%).

When should I choose GPT-5 over GPT-5 Mini?

Choose GPT-5 when: (1) you need the highest quality reasoning and instruction following, (2) your tasks require complex multi-step analysis, (3) cost is less important than output quality. Choose GPT-5 Mini when: (1) you want 80% cost savings, (2) same 272K context window meets your needs, (3) your tasks are well-defined and don't require premium reasoning.

Do GPT-5 and GPT-5 Mini have the same context window?

Yes, both GPT-5 and GPT-5 Mini offer a 272K token context window. The main difference is in reasoning capability and output quality, not context size. GPT-5 delivers premium reasoning for complex tasks, while GPT-5 Mini provides solid performance at 20% of the cost.

How much can I save by switching from GPT-5 to GPT-5 Mini?

You save 80% on both input and output tokens. At 500 requests/day with 1,500 input and 800 output tokens each, GPT-5 costs $28.13/month vs GPT-5 Mini's $5.63/month — saving $22.50/month ($270/year). For high-volume workloads, savings scale linearly.
