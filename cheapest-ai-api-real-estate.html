Real Estate-Specific Considerations

  • Fair Housing Act compliance: AI-generated listing descriptions must not contain discriminatory language or steering. Review all AI outputs for compliance with federal and state fair housing regulations before publishing.
  • Brand voice: Listing descriptions reflect the agent or brokerage brand. Premium models produce more natural, engaging copy that matches human-written style. Budget models work for drafts, but expect to edit for tone.
  • Data privacy: Client information — names, financials, offers — requires secure handling. Use API access with data processing agreements, and never send client PII to providers that train on your data.
  • Multilingual markets: For diverse markets with international buyers, models with strong multilingual support (Gemini, GPT) serve clients in their preferred language without separate translation steps.
  • Market analysis accuracy: For CMA reports and pricing models, premium models with stronger reasoning produce more reliable valuations. Budget models may miss nuances in comparable sales data.
