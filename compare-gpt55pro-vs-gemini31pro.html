Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gemini 3.1 Pro cheaper than GPT-5.5 Pro?

Yes. Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $2.00/M input and $12.00/M output. GPT-5.5 Pro costs $30.00/M input and $180.00/M output. Gemini 3.1 Pro is 93% cheaper on both input and output. For 1M input + 500K output/month, Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $8.00 vs GPT-5.5 Pro's $120.00 — saving $112.00/month (93%).

Which has a larger context window?

GPT-5.5 Pro has a 1.05M token context window — 5% larger than Gemini 3.1 Pro's 1M context. The difference is minimal; both handle very long documents well.

When should I choose GPT-5.5 Pro over Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Choose GPT-5.5 Pro when: (1) you need the absolute highest reasoning capability, (2) budget is not a concern, (3) your workload requires cutting-edge performance. Choose Gemini 3.1 Pro when: (1) cost matters (93% cheaper), (2) you want Google's ecosystem, (3) your workload fits within 1M context.

Are both models production-ready?

Both are production-ready. GPT-5.5 Pro is OpenAI's most powerful model with 1.05M context and top-tier reasoning. Gemini 3.1 Pro is Google's mid-tier model with 1M context and strong capabilities at a fraction of the cost.
