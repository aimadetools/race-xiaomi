How much cheaper is Grok 4.3 vs GPT-5.5 Pro?

Is Grok 4.3 good enough to replace GPT-5.5 Pro?

For many tasks, yes. Grok 4.3 is a strong mid-tier model that handles most coding, analysis, and content generation tasks well. GPT-5.5 Pro excels at the most demanding reasoning and chain-of-thought tasks. When should I choose GPT-5.5 Pro over Grok 4.3?

Choose Grok 4.3 when: (1) you want 97%+ cost savings, (2) mid-tier quality is sufficient, (3) you're optimizing for volume workloads.
