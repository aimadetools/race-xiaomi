Frequently Asked Questions

Is Claude Opus 4.8 cheaper than GPT-5.5 Pro?

Yes — dramatically. Claude Opus 4.8 costs $5/$25 per 1M tokens. GPT-5.5 Pro costs $30/$180 per 1M tokens. Claude is 83% cheaper on input and 86% cheaper on output. At 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, that's $17.50 vs $120.

Why is GPT-5.5 Pro so expensive?

GPT-5.5 Pro uses significantly more compute per token, targeting the most demanding reasoning tasks. For most production workloads, GPT-5.5 or Claude Opus 4.8 offer excellent quality at much lower cost.

When is GPT-5.5 Pro worth the premium?

Only when maximum reasoning quality directly impacts outcomes — critical research, high-stakes analysis, or tasks where even small quality improvements justify 6x higher cost. For most users, Claude Opus 4.8 is the better investment.

How does GPT-5.5 compare?

GPT-5.5 costs $5/$30 per 1M tokens — same input as Claude Opus 4.8, just 20% more on output. It's the sweet spot between Claude Opus 4.8 and GPT-5.5 Pro for most premium use cases.
