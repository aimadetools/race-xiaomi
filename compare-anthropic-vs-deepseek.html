Frequently Asked Questions

Is DeepSeek cheaper than Anthropic for API usage?

Yes, dramatically. DeepSeek V4 Pro ($0.44/$0.87 per 1M tokens) is 11x cheaper on input and 29x cheaper on output than Claude Opus 4.7 ($5/$25). Even at the budget tier, DeepSeek V4 Flash ($0.14/$0.28) is 7x cheaper on input than Claude Haiku 4.5 ($1/$5). DeepSeek offers the lowest prices in the market while maintaining strong performance.

What is the cheapest model between Anthropic and DeepSeek?

DeepSeek V4 Flash at $0.14/$0.28 per 1M tokens is the cheapest option — 7x less than Claude Haiku 4.5 ($1/$5). At 1M tokens/month, DeepSeek V4 Flash costs $4.20 vs Claude Haiku 4.5's $60 — saving $55.80/month. DeepSeek V4 Pro ($0.44/$0.87) is 11x cheaper than Claude Opus 4.7 ($5/$25) for higher-quality tasks.

Can DeepSeek replace Claude for production workloads?

For many use cases, yes. DeepSeek V4 Pro matches or exceeds Claude Sonnet 4.6 on coding benchmarks at 7x lower cost. For general chat, content generation, and data processing, DeepSeek performs well at a fraction of the cost. Choose Anthropic when you need the highest reasoning quality (Opus 4.7), specific safety guarantees, or enterprise compliance. For budget-conscious production workloads, DeepSeek is the clear winner.

Claude Haiku 4.5 vs DeepSeek V4 Flash: which is better?

DeepSeek V4 Flash wins on price by 7x ($0.14 vs $1 input), but Claude Haiku 4.5 generally outperforms on quality benchmarks. For simple tasks like classification, extraction, and short-form chat, DeepSeek V4 Flash is the better value. For tasks requiring higher accuracy or complex reasoning at the budget tier, Claude Haiku 4.5 justifies its premium. DeepSeek V4 Flash also has a 1M context window vs Haiku's 200K.

Claude Opus 4.7 vs DeepSeek V4 Pro: which should I choose?

Claude Opus 4.7 ($5/$25) is Anthropic's most capable model with 1M context. DeepSeek V4 Pro ($0.44/$0.87) is 11x cheaper with the same 1M context. For most tasks, DeepSeek V4 Pro provides excellent value. Choose Opus 4.7 only when you need peak reasoning ability, complex multi-step analysis, or the highest quality outputs. For code generation, summarization, and general tasks, DeepSeek V4 Pro delivers 90% of the quality at 10% of the cost.
