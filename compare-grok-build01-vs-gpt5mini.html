Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is GPT-5 mini than Grok Build 0.1?

Grok Build 0.1 costs $0.30/$0.50 per 1M tokens. GPT-5 mini costs $0.25/$2.00 per 1M tokens.

When should I choose Grok Build 0.1 over GPT-5 mini?

Choose Grok Build 0.1 for output-heavy workloads (content generation, summarization) where the $0.50/1M output price beats GPT-5 mini's $2.00. Choose GPT-5 mini for input-heavy workloads where its $0.25 input price wins.

Is Grok Build 0.1 good for production?

Grok Build 0.1 is xAI's latest budget model with 256K context. It's suitable for production workloads that need good-enough quality at budget pricing. For critical applications, consider mid-tier models like GPT-5 or Claude Sonnet 4.6.
