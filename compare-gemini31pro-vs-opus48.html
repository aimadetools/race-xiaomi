Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gemini 3.1 Pro cheaper than Claude Opus 4.8?

Yes. Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $2/M input and $12/M output. Claude Opus 4.8 costs $5/M input and $25/M output. Gemini is 60% cheaper on input and 52% cheaper on output. For a typical workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, Gemini costs $8.00 vs Opus 4.8's $17.50 — saving you $9.50/month (54%).

How does Gemini 3.1 Pro quality compare to Claude Opus 4.8?

Claude Opus 4.8 is widely regarded as having the strongest reasoning and coding capabilities. Gemini 3.1 Pro offers excellent quality at 52-60% lower cost with the same 1M context window. For most production workloads, Gemini 3.1 Pro delivers comparable results at significantly lower cost. Opus 4.8 excels at the most demanding tasks.

When should I choose Claude Opus 4.8 over Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Choose Claude Opus 4.8 when you need: (1) absolute best reasoning quality, (2) complex multi-step analysis, (3) nuanced writing and creative tasks, (4) enterprise safety features. Choose Gemini 3.1 Pro when you want excellent quality at 52-60% lower cost with the same 1M context window — ideal for production workloads where cost matters.

Do both models support 1M context windows?

Yes. Both Gemini 3.1 Pro and Claude Opus 4.8 offer 1M token context windows. This makes the comparison purely about price and quality preferences, since context capacity is identical. Gemini 3.1 Pro delivers this at 52-60% lower cost.
