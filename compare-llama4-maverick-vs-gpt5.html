Frequently Asked Questions

Is Llama 4 Maverick cheaper than GPT-5?

Yes, significantly. Llama 4 Maverick costs $0.27/M input and $0.85/M output. GPT-5 costs $1.25/M input and $10.00/M output. Llama 4 Maverick is 78% cheaper on input and 91% cheaper on output. For a typical workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, Llama 4 Maverick costs $0.70 vs GPT-5's $6.25 — saving you $5.55/month (89%).

How does Llama 4 Maverick quality compare to GPT-5?

GPT-5 is generally stronger at complex reasoning and creative tasks. Llama 4 Maverick is competitive for coding, instruction-following, and most production workloads at 89% lower cost. For many real-world applications, the quality difference does not justify the 9x price premium of GPT-5.

Can I self-host Llama 4 Maverick?

Yes. Llama 4 Maverick is open-source under MIT license. You can self-host via Together.ai, AWS, or your own infrastructure for near-zero marginal cost at scale. This is a major advantage over GPT-5, which is proprietary and can only be accessed through OpenAI's API.

When should I choose GPT-5 over Llama 4 Maverick?

Choose GPT-5 when you need top-tier complex reasoning, OpenAI's ecosystem and plugins, or don't want to manage infrastructure. GPT-5 also has stronger brand recognition for customer-facing applications. Choose Llama 4 Maverick when cost efficiency and self-hosting flexibility matter most.
