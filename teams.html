Frequently Asked Questions

How much can my team save on AI API costs?
Teams typically save 30-60% by switching to cost-optimized models. For a team of 10 developers making 500 requests/day, that can mean $2,000-$5,000/month in savings depending on current provider and model choices.
Do I need an account to use the team tools?
No. All team tools are free to use with no account required. Enter your team's usage data and get instant cost comparisons, migration plans, and vendor evaluations. Pro ($29 one-time) unlocks PDF exports for finance reports and detailed migration guides.
How accurate are the cost estimates?
Pricing data is verified monthly against official provider documentation. We track 49 models across 10 providers including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, Mistral, and more. Estimates are based on published per-token rates and typical usage patterns.
Can I generate a report for my CFO or finance team?
Yes. The Team Cost Planner generates a detailed budget breakdown with per-developer costs, model comparisons, and projected annual spend. Pro users can export this as a formatted PDF ready for finance review.
What if my team uses multiple AI providers?
All three tools support multi-provider analysis. The Vendor Scorecard lets you compare up to 8 providers side-by-side with weighted scoring. The Migration Planner handles cross-provider switches (e.g., OpenAI to Anthropic) with provider-specific code snippets.
Is this just for large teams?
No. Even a 2-person team can benefit — the tools scale from solo developers to enterprises with 200+ engineers. The smaller the team, the more each dollar saved matters.
