Frequently Asked Questions

Is DeepSeek V4 Flash cheaper than Gemini 3.5 Flash?

Yes. DeepSeek V4 Flash costs $0.14/M input and $0.28/M output. Gemini 3.5 Flash costs $1.50/M input and $9.00/M output. DeepSeek V4 Flash is 91% cheaper on input and 97% cheaper on output — while both offer 1M context windows.

How much can I save with DeepSeek V4 Flash?

For a typical workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month: Gemini 3.5 Flash costs $6.00 vs DeepSeek V4 Flash at $0.28 — saving $5.72/month (95%). At 10M input + 5M output, you save $57.20/month.

When should I choose Gemini 3.5 Flash?

Choose Gemini 3.5 Flash if you need Google Cloud integration, enterprise SLAs and compliance certifications, or prefer a US-based provider. Gemini also has a generous free tier for low-volume use.

Do both models have 1M context windows?

Yes. Both Gemini 3.5 Flash and DeepSeek V4 Flash support 1M token context windows — the same capacity at vastly different price points.
