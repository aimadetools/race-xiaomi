Key Takeaways

The 150x Gap

The cheapest model (Mistral Small at $0.000150/request) costs 150x less than the most expensive (GPT-5.5 Pro at $0.022500/request) for a 1K-token request. At 5K tokens, the gap holds at 150x.

  • For high-volume chatbots: Mistral Small, Gemini Flash, or DeepSeek V4 Flash — all under $0.001 per 1K-token request
  • For production code assistants: GPT-4o or Claude Sonnet 4 — $0.0025–$0.003 per request with strong reasoning
  • For complex research: Claude 4 Opus or GPT-5 — $0.01–$0.015 per request, but best-in-class quality
  • Best value in mid-tier: Llama 3.1 70B at $0.00088/request — 3x cheaper than GPT-4o with comparable quality
  • Hidden winner: Gemini 2.5 Pro at $0.0025/request — strong reasoning with 1M context window

How to Use This Table

These costs assume a 3:1 input-to-output token ratio. Your actual costs depend on your specific workload:

  • Chatbots: Usually 2:1 to 4:1 ratio — this table is accurate
  • Code generation: Often 1:3 or higher — output-heavy, so multiply output costs
  • Document analysis: Often 10:1 or higher — input-heavy, costs are lower than shown
  • RAG pipelines: Usually 5:1 — input-heavy with retrieved context
