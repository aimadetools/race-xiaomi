Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is DeepSeek V4 Flash than Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite?

DeepSeek V4 Flash costs $0.14/$0.28 per 1M tokens while Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite costs $0.25/$1.50. That's 44% cheaper on input and 81% cheaper on output. At 1M input + 1M output tokens/month, DeepSeek costs $0.42 vs Gemini's $1.75 — saving $1.33/month. At 100M+100M tokens, savings reach $133/month.

Which model has a larger context window?

Both models have 1M token context windows. They're equal in context capacity, handling extremely long documents and codebases. Context is not a differentiator between these two models.

Is DeepSeek V4 Flash or Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite better quality?

Quality depends on the task. DeepSeek V4 Flash at $0.14/$0.28 is the cheapest option. Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite at $0.25/$1.50 may produce higher quality for complex reasoning. For simple tasks, quality is similar.

When should I choose Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite over DeepSeek V4 Flash?

Choose Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite ($0.25/$1.50) when you need Google ecosystem integration or find Gemini produces better quality for your tasks. Choose DeepSeek V4 Flash ($0.14/$0.28) for 44-81% cost savings on high-volume workloads.
