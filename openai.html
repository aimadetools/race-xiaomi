Which OpenAI Model Should You Use?

Match your use case to the right model and tier.

Chatbots & Customer Support

GPT-4o mini

Fast, affordable, and good enough for most conversational tasks. At $0.15/$0.60 per 1M tokens, it handles FAQs and simple support queries at minimal cost.

Content Generation & Marketing

GPT-4o

Excellent balance of quality and cost. Produces high-quality long-form content, blog posts, and marketing copy with strong reasoning at $2.50/$10.00 per 1M tokens.

Data Analysis & Summarization

GPT-5 mini

Modern reasoning with 256K context at a budget price. Ideal for analyzing large documents, summarizing reports, and processing structured data at $0.40/$1.60.

Code Generation & Debugging

GPT-4o

Strong coding performance with 128K context. Handles multi-file codebases, reviews, and refactoring reliably for $2.50/$10.00 per 1M tokens.

Complex Reasoning & Research

GPT-5

OpenAI's most capable model. Best for multi-step reasoning, complex analysis, and tasks requiring the highest accuracy. 256K context at $10.00/$30.00.

Bulk Processing & ETL

GPT-3.5 Turbo

The cheapest OpenAI option for high-volume, simple tasks. Good for classification, extraction, and lightweight processing at $0.50/$1.50 per 1M tokens.
