Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mistral Small 4 cheaper than Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Yes, significantly. Mistral Small 4 costs $0.10/$0.30 per 1M tokens while Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $2.00/$12.00. That's 95% cheaper on input and 97.5% cheaper on output. For high-volume workloads, Mistral Small 4 can be 40x cheaper overall.

Which has a larger context window?

Gemini 3.1 Pro has a 1M token context window, 7.8x larger than Mistral Small 4's 128K context window. This makes Gemini 3.1 Pro better for processing long documents, large codebases, and multimodal tasks that require extensive context.

When should I choose Gemini 3.1 Pro over Mistral Small 4?

Choose Gemini 3.1 Pro when you need multimodal capabilities, long context processing, or Google ecosystem integration. Gemini 3.1 Pro excels at processing images, video, and audio alongside text, with a full 1M context window.

When should I choose Mistral Small 4 over Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Choose Mistral Small 4 when cost is your primary concern and your tasks don't require multimodal capabilities or the full 1M context window. Mistral Small 4 is ideal for text-only high-volume workloads at $0.10/$0.30 per 1M tokens.

How much can I save switching from Gemini 3.1 Pro to Mistral Small 4?

Savings are significant. At 1M tokens/month (50% input, 50% output), Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $70.00 while Mistral Small 4 costs $0.20 — saving $69.80/month (99.7%). At 100M tokens/month, you'd save $6,980/month. The tradeoff is multimodal capability and context window size.

The Verdict
