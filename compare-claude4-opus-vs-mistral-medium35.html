Is Mistral Medium 3.5 cheaper than Claude 4 Opus?

Yes, dramatically. Claude 4 Opus costs $15.00/M input and $75.00/M output. Mistral Medium 3.5 costs $1.50/M input and $7.50/M output. Mistral Medium 3.5 is 90% cheaper on both input and output. How does Mistral Medium 3.5 quality compare to Claude 4 Opus?

Claude 4 Opus was Anthropic's flagship model with excellent reasoning and coding capabilities, but it was retired on June 15, 2026. Mistral Medium 3.5 from Mistral AI is a strong mid-tier competitor with a 128K token context window. Mistral Medium 3.5 excels at code generation, instruction following, and multilingual tasks.
