Frequently Asked Questions

Is Grok 4.3 cheaper than Claude Sonnet 4.6?

Yes. Grok 4.3 is significantly cheaper: $1.25/M input (58% cheaper than Claude Sonnet 4.6's $3.00/M) and $2.50/M output (83% cheaper than Claude Sonnet 4.6's $15.00/M). Both have 1M context windows.

When would I choose Claude Sonnet 4.6?

Choose Claude Sonnet 4.6 if you need Anthropic's safety-focused approach, superior instruction following, or enterprise-grade content moderation. Claude also offers stronger performance on complex reasoning tasks.

Do both models have 1M context windows?

Yes. Both Grok 4.3 and Claude Sonnet 4.6 support 1M token context windows, making them suitable for long documents, codebases, and extended conversations.

Which is better for production workloads?

Grok 4.3 offers dramatically lower costs — up to 83% savings on output-heavy tasks. Claude Sonnet 4.6 provides Anthropic's enterprise infrastructure and safety guarantees. For cost-sensitive production, Grok wins. For enterprise compliance, Claude may be preferred.
