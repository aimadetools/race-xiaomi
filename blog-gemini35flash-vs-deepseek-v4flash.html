Where Gemini 3.5 Flash Wins

  • Google Cloud integration — Native support for Vertex AI, BigQuery ML, and Google's ecosystem. If you're already on GCP, the integration overhead is zero.
  • Enterprise compliance — SOC 2, HIPAA, and FedRAMP certifications. Google's enterprise SLAs and data residency options are more mature.
  • Free tier — Gemini offers a generous free tier for testing and prototyping. DeepSeek's free tier is more limited.
  • Multimodal capabilities — Gemini's native vision and audio processing is more mature than DeepSeek's.
  • US-based provider — For regulated industries, a US-based provider may be required.

Where DeepSeek V4 Flash Wins

  • Price — 95-97% cheaper across the board. At $0.14/$0.28, it's one of the cheapest AI models available.
  • Same context window — 1M tokens, identical to Gemini 3.5 Flash. No capability trade-off for the price drop.
  • Cost predictability — Simple, transparent pricing with no hidden fees or tier-based pricing changes.
  • API simplicity — OpenAI-compatible API format. Drop-in replacement for GPT-4o-mini or similar models.
