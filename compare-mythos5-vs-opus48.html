Is Claude Mythos 5 or Claude Opus 4.8 cheaper?

What's the difference between Claude Mythos 5 and Claude Opus 4.8?

Mythos 5 is Anthropic's premium creative model at $10/$50 per 1M tokens, optimized for creative writing, narrative reasoning, and nuanced analysis. Opus 4.8 is Anthropic's general-purpose flagship at $5/$25 per 1M tokens — half the price — with 1M context and strong all-around performance. Choose Mythos 5 for creative tasks, Opus 4.8 for cost-effective general use.

Why would I pay more for Mythos 5 when Opus 4.8 is half the price?

Mythos 5's premium pricing reflects its specialized training for creative and narrative tasks. If your workload involves heavy creative writing, marketing copy, storytelling, or nuanced narrative analysis, Mythos 5 produces higher-quality output for those tasks. For general-purpose coding, analysis, and everyday AI tasks, Opus 4.8 at half the price is the smarter choice.

Which is better for coding: Claude Mythos 5 or Opus 4.8?

Claude Opus 4.8 is the better choice for coding at half the price. It's a strong general-purpose model that handles code generation, debugging, and architecture work well. Mythos 5 can code too, but its strength is creative and narrative tasks — you'd be paying a 2x premium for a model specialized in areas that aren't code.
