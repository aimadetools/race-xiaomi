The Verdict

GPT-5.6 Sol is the clear value winner. GPT-5.4 Pro only makes sense for niche use cases.

GPT-5.6 Sol ($5/$30) costs 83% less than GPT-5.4 Pro ($30/$180) while offering the same 1.05M context window. For most workloads, Sol delivers sufficient performance at a fraction of the cost. Only consider GPT-5.4 Pro if you need its enhanced reasoning capabilities for extremely complex tasks — and even then, test whether Sol handles your workload adequately before committing.

When to Choose GPT-5.6 Sol

  • Cost-sensitive production: At $5/$30, Sol is 83% cheaper than Pro. For high-volume APIs, this saves thousands per month.
  • Standard workloads: Chatbots, code generation, summarization, translation — Sol handles these at a fraction of Pro's cost.
  • Startup budgets: If you're building an AI startup, Sol gives you 6x the requests for the same budget vs Pro.

When to Choose GPT-5.4 Pro

  • Extreme reasoning complexity: If your tasks require the absolute highest reasoning capabilities and Sol produces inadequate results.
  • Research & evaluation: For benchmarking or tasks where the Pro tier's enhanced reasoning justifies the 6x cost premium.
  • Critical applications: When the cost of errors exceeds the cost premium — e.g., financial analysis, legal reasoning, or safety-critical systems.
