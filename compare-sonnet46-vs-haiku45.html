Frequently Asked Questions

Is Claude Haiku 4.5 cheaper than Sonnet 4.6?

Yes. Claude Haiku 4.5 costs $1.00/M input and $5.00/M output — 67% cheaper on both input and output than Claude Sonnet 4.6's $3.00/M input and $15.00/M output.

When would I choose Sonnet 4.6 over Haiku 4.5?

Choose Sonnet 4.6 for complex reasoning, nuanced analysis, longer-form content generation, and tasks requiring deeper understanding. Sonnet has 5× more context (1M vs 200K) and significantly better performance on complex benchmarks.

What's the context window difference?

Is Haiku 4.5 good enough for production?
