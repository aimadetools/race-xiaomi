Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gemini 2.5 Pro cheaper than Claude Sonnet 4.6?

Yes. Gemini 2.5 Pro costs $1.25/M input and $10.00/M output. Claude Sonnet 4.6 costs $3.00/M input and $15.00/M output. Gemini 2.5 Pro is 58% cheaper on input and 33% cheaper on output. For a typical workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, Gemini 2.5 Pro costs $6.25 vs Claude Sonnet 4.6's $10.50 — saving you $4.25/month (40%).

How does Gemini 2.5 Pro quality compare to Claude Sonnet 4.6?

Both are excellent mid-tier models with 1M token context windows. Claude Sonnet 4.6 is stronger at creative writing, nuanced tasks, and follows instructions with greater precision. Gemini 2.5 Pro excels at multimodal tasks with native support for images, audio, and video. For most production use cases, both deliver competitive quality at different price points.

When should I choose Claude Sonnet 4.6 over Gemini 2.5 Pro?

Choose Claude Sonnet 4.6 when you need: (1) Anthropic's industry-leading safety features and alignment, (2) superior creative writing and nuanced analysis, (3) enterprise SLAs and compliance certifications from Anthropic. Choose Gemini 2.5 Pro when cost efficiency matters most, you need native multimodal capabilities, or your use case involves Google ecosystem integration.

Do both models support the same context window?

Yes. Both Gemini 2.5 Pro and Claude Sonnet 4.6 offer 1M token context windows, making them suitable for long-document processing, large codebases, and complex multi-turn conversations. This is a strong shared capability at the mid-tier level.
