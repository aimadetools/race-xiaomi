Is Claude Sonnet 4.6 cheaper than GPT-5.5 Pro?

When should I choose GPT-5.5 Pro over Sonnet 4.6?

Choose GPT-5.5 Pro when: (1) you need the absolute best reasoning capabilities, (2) cost is not a primary concern, (3) you need enhanced chain-of-thought for complex multi-step problems. Choose Sonnet 4.6 when: (1) you want 90% cost savings, (2) similar context windows (1.05M vs 1M) meet your needs, (3) you need strong instruction following at a fraction of the price.

Are GPT-5.5 Pro and Sonnet 4.6 production-ready?

Both are production-ready. GPT-5.5 Pro is OpenAI's premium reasoning model — overkill for most use cases but unmatched for complex analytical tasks. Claude Sonnet 4.6 is Anthropic's balanced mid-tier model, offering excellent quality at 10% of the cost. For most production workloads, Sonnet 4.6 provides 85-95% of the quality at a fraction of the price.
