When should I choose Gemini 3.5 Flash over DeepSeek V4 Pro?

Choose Gemini 3.5 Flash when you need Google's ecosystem integration (Vertex AI, BigQuery, Google Cloud), proven multimodal capabilities, or the reliability of a Google-backed service. Choose DeepSeek V4 Pro when cost is your primary concern.
