Frequently Asked Questions

Is Claude Fable 5 or Gemini 3.1 Pro cheaper?

Gemini 3.1 Pro is much cheaper. It costs $2.00/$12.00 per 1M tokens while Claude Fable 5 costs $10.00/$50.00. That's 80% cheaper on input and 76% cheaper on output. At 1M tokens/month, Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $42 vs Fable 5's $180.

What is Claude Fable 5 used for?

Claude Fable 5 at $10/$50 is Anthropic's premium creative and storytelling model, designed for complex narrative generation, creative writing, character development, and tasks requiring sophisticated language understanding. It's 3.3x more expensive than Sonnet 5 and 5x more than Gemini 3.1 Pro.

When should I use Fable 5 vs Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Fable 5 ($10/$50) is for specialized creative tasks requiring premium narrative capability — complex storytelling, creative writing, character-driven content. Gemini 3.1 Pro ($2/$12) handles 90% of general AI tasks at 80% lower cost. Most users should start with Gemini 3.1 Pro and only use Fable 5 for creative work where quality is critical.

How does Fable 5 compare to Sonnet 5?

Fable 5 ($10/$50) costs 3.3x more than Sonnet 5 ($3/$15). Fable 5 specializes in creative and narrative tasks while Sonnet 5 is a general-purpose model. For most tasks, Sonnet 5 offers better value. Choose Fable 5 specifically for creative writing and storytelling workloads.

Can I use Fable 5 and Gemini 3.1 Pro together?

Yes! A cost-effective pattern: use Gemini 3.1 Pro ($2/$12) for 90% of tasks (analysis, coding, general queries) and route only creative writing and narrative tasks to Fable 5 ($10/$50). This gives you premium creative capability where needed while keeping costs 80% lower for standard workloads.
