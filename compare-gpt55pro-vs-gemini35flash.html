Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is Gemini 3.5 Flash vs GPT-5.5 Pro?

Is Gemini 3.5 Flash good enough to replace GPT-5.5 Pro?

For many tasks, yes. Gemini 3.5 Flash handles chatbots, content generation, summarization, and RAG pipelines well at a fraction of the cost. However, GPT-5.5 Pro excels at complex multi-step reasoning, advanced coding, and tasks requiring the highest accuracy. The right choice depends on your quality requirements and budget.

When should I choose GPT-5.5 Pro over Gemini 3.5 Flash?

Choose GPT-5.5 Pro when: (1) you need maximum reasoning power and chain-of-thought accuracy, (2) your task requires complex multi-step analysis, (3) output quality is more important than cost. Choose Gemini 3.5 Flash when: (1) cost optimization is a priority, (2) you're handling high-volume requests, (3) good-enough quality meets your needs.

What's the cost difference at scale?
