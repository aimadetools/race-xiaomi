1. Log Your Monthly Usage

Select your current model from our database of 67 models, enter your monthly input and output tokens, and hit save. That's it.

2. See Your Spending History

Every month, log your usage again. The dashboard shows a visual chart of your spending over time — are you spending more or less than last month?

3. Discover Cheaper Alternatives

The dashboard automatically calculates how much you'd save by switching to cheaper models. For your exact usage pattern, not generic estimates.

4. It's Completely Private

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. No account needed, no data sent to our servers. Your spending data stays on your machine.

How to Get Started

  1. Go to My Cost Dashboard
  2. Select your current model (e.g., GPT-5.4, Claude Sonnet 5)
  3. Enter your monthly token usage (input and output)
  4. Click "Save to My Dashboard"
  5. Check back next month to see your trend

That's it. No account, no signup, no credit card. Just honest cost tracking.
