Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is Kimi K2.6?

Dramatically cheaper. Opus 4.8 costs $5/$25 per 1M tokens vs Kimi K2.6 at $0.95/$4 — 81% cheaper on input, 84% cheaper on output. At 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, that's $17.50 vs $2.95, saving $14.55/month. At 10M input + 5M output, the savings reach $145.50/month.

Can Kimi K2.6 match Opus quality?

No. Opus 4.8 is Anthropic's flagship with the strongest reasoning, coding, and instruction-following. Kimi K2.6 at $0.95/$4 handles moderate tasks well but falls short on complex reasoning, multi-step coding, and nuanced analysis. The quality gap is significant for demanding work.

When is Opus worth the premium?

When quality directly impacts outcomes: complex research, critical code generation, AI agents with multi-step reasoning, legal or medical analysis, and tasks where errors are costly. At $5/$25 per 1M tokens, Opus 4.8 delivers measurably better results for high-stakes work.

What about context window differences?

Opus 4.8 has a 1M token context window — 4x larger than Kimi K2.6's 256K. This matters for long documents, large codebases, or extended conversations. If your inputs exceed 256K tokens, Kimi K2.6 won't work.
