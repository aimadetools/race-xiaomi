How much cheaper is DeepSeek V4 Pro than Gemini 3 Flash?

DeepSeek V4 Pro costs $0.435/$0.87 per 1M tokens. Gemini 3 Flash costs $0.50/$3.00 per 1M tokens. DeepSeek is 13% cheaper on input and 71% cheaper on output — a typical workload saves $1.13/month (57% overall).

Do they have the same context window?

Yes — both offer 1M token context windows. This makes them two of the cheapest ways to access 1M context in 2026. The context capability is equivalent.

When should I choose DeepSeek V4 Pro over Gemini 3 Flash?

Choose DeepSeek V4 Pro when output cost matters most — it's 71% cheaper on output tokens, ideal for content generation and code completion. Choose Gemini 3 Flash for Google ecosystem integration or multimodal tasks.

Is DeepSeek V4 Pro as good as Gemini 3 Flash?

Both are strong budget models with 1M context. DeepSeek excels at coding and technical tasks. Gemini benefits from Google's ecosystem. For most text-based workloads, quality is comparable — making DeepSeek's 57% savings hard to beat.
