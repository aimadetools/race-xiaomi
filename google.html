Why Gemini Stands Out

Three reasons Google's API is hard to beat.

1M

1M Context Window

Gemini 2.5 Pro and Flash both support up to 1 million tokens of context — far beyond what OpenAI or Anthropic offer. Process entire codebases, long documents, or massive conversations in a single request.

$

Cheapest Premium Model

Gemini 2.5 Pro at $1.25/$10.00 per 1M tokens is significantly cheaper than GPT-4o ($2.50/$10.00) and Claude Sonnet 4 ($3.00/$15.00) while delivering comparable intelligence.

Free

Free Tier Available

Google offers a generous free tier for both Gemini 2.5 Pro and Gemini 2.0 Flash, letting you experiment and prototype without spending a dime. Perfect for side projects and evaluation.

Which Gemini Model Should You Use?

Gemini 2.5 Pro
Best for complex reasoning, code generation, multi-step tasks, and analysis that benefits from a large context window. Choose this when accuracy matters more than cost — and still get it at a lower price than GPT-4o.
Gemini 2.0 Flash
Best for high-volume, latency-sensitive workloads: chatbots, content classification, translation, summarization, and rapid prototyping. At $0.10/$0.40 per 1M tokens, it's the cheapest model with a 1M context window on the market.
