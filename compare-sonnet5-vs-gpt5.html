Content Generation

Blog posts, marketing copy, documentation. GPT-5 at $1.25/$10 is 33% cheaper on output, making it ideal for bulk content. Sonnet 5 at $3/$15 produces more nuanced prose for premium content.

Complex Analysis

Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is GPT-5 than Sonnet 5?

GPT-5 costs $1.25/$10 per 1M tokens vs Sonnet 5 at $3/$15 — 58% cheaper on input, 33% cheaper on output. At 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, that's $6.25 vs $10.50, saving $4.25/month. At 10M input + 5M output, savings reach $42.50/month.

Is Sonnet 5 better than GPT-5?

Sonnet 5 ($3/$15) is newer with slightly better code generation and reasoning. GPT-5 ($1.25/$10) is more cost-effective at 58% lower input cost. For most workloads, GPT-5 offers better value. Choose Sonnet 5 when you need the latest Anthropic capabilities or are in the Anthropic ecosystem.

Does Sonnet 5 have introductory pricing?

Yes. Claude Sonnet 5 offers introductory pricing of $2/$10 per MTok through August 31, 2026 — 33% cheaper than standard. After August 31, it returns to $3/$15. GPT-5 has no announced introductory pricing at $1.25/$10.
