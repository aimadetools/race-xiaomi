Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mistral Small 4 cheaper than GPT-5.4 mini?

Yes, Mistral Small 4 is dramatically cheaper. It costs $0.15/$0.60 per 1M tokens while GPT-5.4 mini costs $0.75/$4.50. That's 87% cheaper on input and 93% cheaper on output. At 1M tokens/month, Mistral Small 4 costs $0.40 vs GPT-5.4 mini's $5.25 — saving $4.85/month.

How much can I save switching from GPT-5.4 mini to Mistral Small 4?

You can save up to 90%+ on your AI API costs by switching to Mistral Small 4. Input tokens are 87% cheaper ($0.10 vs $0.75) and output tokens are 93% cheaper ($0.30 vs $4.50). For a typical workload of 1M input + 500K output tokens per month, you'd save about $4.85/month — that's a 92% reduction.

Is Mistral Small 4 good enough for production?

Yes, Mistral Small 4 is production-ready and widely used for chatbots, code generation, and data processing. It handles most standard tasks well at a fraction of the cost. While GPT-5.4 mini may have an edge on some complex reasoning tasks, Mistral Small 4 is an excellent value for production workloads that don't require cutting-edge capabilities.

Which has a bigger context window: GPT-5.4 mini or Mistral Small 4?

GPT-5.4 mini has a 400K token context window, while Mistral Small 4 has a 128K token context window. GPT-5.4 mini supports 3x more context, which is critical for long document analysis, large codebases, and complex multi-step reasoning tasks.

Should I use GPT-5.4 mini or Mistral Small 4 for my chatbot?

For most chatbot use cases, Mistral Small 4 is the better choice. It's 87% cheaper on input and 93% cheaper on output, which matters a lot at scale. It handles conversational AI, customer support, and FAQ-style queries well. Choose GPT-5.4 mini only if you need the larger 400K context window or are locked into the OpenAI ecosystem.
