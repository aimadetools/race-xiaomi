Frequently Asked Questions

Which is cheaper, DeepSeek V4 Flash or Mistral Small 4?

It depends on your usage pattern. Mistral Small 4 is 29% cheaper on input ($0.10/M vs $0.14/M), while DeepSeek V4 Flash is 7% cheaper on output ($0.28/M vs $0.30/M). For input-heavy workloads, Mistral wins; for output-heavy, DeepSeek wins.

When should I choose DeepSeek V4 Flash?

Choose DeepSeek V4 Flash for long context needs (1M vs 128K), output-heavy workloads where its 7% cheaper output adds up, and Chinese language support where DeepSeek excels.

When should I choose Mistral Small 4?

Choose Mistral Small 4 for input-heavy workloads where its 29% cheaper input matters, European data sovereignty needs (GDPR compliance), and simple tasks where the lower input cost dominates.

Do they both support function calling?

Yes, both DeepSeek V4 Flash and Mistral Small 4 support function calling and tool use, making them suitable for agentic workflows and structured output generation.
