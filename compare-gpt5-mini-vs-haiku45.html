Frequently Asked Questions

Is GPT-5 mini cheaper than Claude Haiku 4.5?

Yes, significantly. GPT-5 mini costs $0.25/$2.00 per 1M tokens while Haiku 4.5 costs $1.00/$5.00. That's 75% cheaper on input and 60% cheaper on output. For output-heavy workloads like chat, GPT-5 mini can be 2.5x cheaper overall.

Which has a larger context window?

GPT-5 mini has a 272K token context window, 36% larger than Claude Haiku 4.5's 200K context window. This makes GPT-5 mini slightly better for long document processing and RAG pipelines that need more context, while still being significantly cheaper.

When should I choose Claude Haiku 4.5 over GPT-5 mini?

Choose Claude Haiku 4.5 when you prioritize reasoning quality over cost, need Anthropic's constitutional AI safety features, or require specific compliance certifications. Haiku 4.5 may produce more nuanced, careful responses on complex tasks despite the higher price.

How much can I save switching from Haiku 4.5 to GPT-5 mini?

At 1M tokens/month (50% input, 50% output), Haiku 4.5 costs $300 while GPT-5 mini costs $112.50 — saving $187.50/month (63%). For output-heavy chat workloads, savings can reach 60%.

Is GPT-5 mini good enough for production use?

Yes, GPT-5 mini is production-ready for budget-tier workloads. It offers better value with lower costs and larger context. However, Haiku 4.5 may excel on reasoning-heavy tasks, safety-sensitive applications, or when you need Anthropic's specific compliance certifications.
