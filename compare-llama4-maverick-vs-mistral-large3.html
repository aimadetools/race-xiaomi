Frequently Asked Questions

Is Llama 4 Maverick cheaper than Mistral Large 3?

Yes. Llama 4 Maverick costs $0.27/$0.85 per 1M tokens. Mistral Large 3 costs $0.50/$1.50 per 1M tokens. Llama is 46% cheaper on input and 43% cheaper on output.

Which has a larger context window?

Llama 4 Maverick offers 1M tokens vs Mistral Large 3's 262K. For long documents or extended conversations, Llama 4 Maverick has a significant advantage.

Can I self-host these models?

Yes! Both have open weights. Llama 4 Maverick via Meta's license, Mistral Large 3 via Mistral's open license. Self-host on cloud GPUs or your own infrastructure.

How do these compare to DeepSeek V4 Flash?

DeepSeek V4 Flash costs $0.14/$0.28 per 1M tokens — even cheaper than both. However, Llama 4 Maverick has 1M context (vs 1M for DeepSeek V4 Flash) and is backed by Meta's ecosystem.
