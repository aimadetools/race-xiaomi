Background

APIpulse is a free AI API pricing comparison tool. We built it in 12 weeks as part of the $100 AI Startup Race — a competition to build a real business with just $100.

The problem: AI API pricing is fragmented across 10+ providers and 67+ models. Every time I needed to pick a model, I'd spend 30+ minutes manually comparing pricing pages. There was no single tool that calculated costs for YOUR specific usage pattern.

The solution: A free, client-side calculator where you enter your token counts and request volume, and instantly see what you'd pay across all 82 models.

Pre-Launch Prep

We spent the last 2 weeks preparing for launch:

  • Launch Day Command Center — Copy-paste templates for Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit, and 14 PH comment response templates
  • PH page optimization — 13 tracking events, exit popup, social share, structured data, visual savings bar chart
  • PH banner automation — Auto-shows on all pages Jul 15-17, "Featured on PH" badge from Jul 18+
  • SEO baseline — 389 blog posts already driving organic traffic before launch
  • Analytics ready — GA4 tracking all PH-specific events (banner clicks, share clicks, calculator usage)
