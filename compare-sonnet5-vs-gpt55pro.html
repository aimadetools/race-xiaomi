Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is Claude Sonnet 5?

Sonnet 5 costs $3/$15 per 1M tokens vs GPT-5.5 Pro at $30/$180 — 90% cheaper on input and 92% cheaper on output. At 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, that's $10.50 vs $120, saving $109.50/month. At 10M input + 5M output, savings reach $1,095/month.

Is GPT-5.5 Pro worth 10x the cost?

GPT-5.5 Pro at $30/$180 is OpenAI's most capable model for the most demanding tasks. For the absolute most complex reasoning, the 10x premium may justify itself in quality. For most production workloads, Sonnet 5 at $3/$15 offers excellent value with comparable quality.

When should I use Sonnet 5 over GPT-5.5 Pro?

Choose Sonnet 5 ($3/$15) for virtually all production workloads: code generation, content creation, data processing, chatbots. Choose GPT-5.5 Pro ($30/$180) only for the most complex multi-step reasoning and tasks where absolute peak quality justifies 10x higher cost.

What are the context window differences?

Claude Sonnet 5 supports 1M token context windows. GPT-5.5 Pro supports 1.05M tokens. The difference is negligible — both handle very large inputs. The real difference is in pricing and model quality.

Does Sonnet 5 have introductory pricing?

Yes. Claude Sonnet 5 offers introductory pricing of $2/$10 per MTok through August 31, 2026 — 93% cheaper than GPT-5.5 Pro's standard pricing. After August 31, Sonnet 5 returns to $3/$15. GPT-5.5 Pro has no introductory pricing at $30/$180.
