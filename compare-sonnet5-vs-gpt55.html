Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is Claude Sonnet 5 than GPT-5.5?

Sonnet 5 costs $3/$15 per 1M tokens vs GPT-5.5 at $5/$30 — 40% cheaper on input, 50% cheaper on output. At 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, that's $10.50 vs $20, saving $9.50/month. At 10M input + 5M output, savings reach $95/month.

Is GPT-5.5 worth the extra cost?

GPT-5.5 at $5/$30 offers the highest OpenAI quality with 1.05M context. Sonnet 5 at $3/$15 delivers comparable quality for 40-50% less. For most production workloads, Sonnet 5 offers better value. Choose GPT-5.5 when you need absolute peak performance or OpenAI ecosystem integration.

Does Sonnet 5 have introductory pricing?

Yes. Claude Sonnet 5 offers introductory pricing of $2/$10 per MTok through August 31, 2026 — 33% cheaper than standard. After August 31, it returns to $3/$15. GPT-5.5 has no announced introductory pricing at $5/$30.

Which has a larger context window?

GPT-5.5 has a slightly larger context at 1.05M tokens vs Sonnet 5's 1M tokens. Both support over 1M tokens, making them directly comparable for long-document processing. The 50K token difference is negligible for most use cases.
