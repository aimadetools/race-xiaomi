Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is DeepSeek V4 Pro?

Significantly cheaper. DeepSeek V4 Pro costs $0.435/$0.87 per 1M tokens vs Sonnet 4.6 at $3/$15 — 85% cheaper on input, 94% cheaper on output. At 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, that's $0.87 vs $10.50, saving $9.63/month. At 10M input + 5M output, savings reach $96.30/month.

Is DeepSeek as good as Sonnet?

DeepSeek V4 Pro delivers impressive quality for its price. However, Sonnet 4.6 at $3/$15 still outperforms on complex reasoning, instruction-following edge cases, and nuanced analysis. For straightforward tasks, DeepSeek is excellent value. For demanding work, Sonnet's quality justifies the premium.

When should I pick Sonnet over DeepSeek?

Pick Sonnet 4.6 ($3/$15) when quality matters: complex coding, research analysis, nuanced content, and tasks requiring strict instruction adherence. Pick DeepSeek V4 Pro ($0.435/$0.87) for batch processing, translation, chatbots, and high-volume tasks where 85-94% lower cost is the priority.

Do they have the same context window?

Yes. Both DeepSeek V4 Pro and Sonnet 4.6 support 1M token context windows. This makes them directly comparable for long-document processing, large codebases, and extended conversations. Both significantly outperform budget models like Kimi K2.6 (256K) on context.
