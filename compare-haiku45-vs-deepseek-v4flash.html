Is DeepSeek V4 Flash cheaper than Claude Haiku 4.5?

Yes, dramatically. DeepSeek V4 Flash costs $0.14/M input and $0.28/M output. Claude Haiku 4.5 costs $1.00/M input and $5.00/M output. When should I choose Claude Haiku 4.5 over DeepSeek V4 Flash?

Choose Claude Haiku 4.5 when: (1) you want Anthropic's strong instruction following and safety features, (2) you need reliable structured output and classification, (3) you prefer a US-based provider with data residency guarantees. Choose DeepSeek V4 Flash when: (1) cost is the primary concern (86-94% cheaper), (2) you're building high-volume applications where cost efficiency matters most, (3) you're comfortable with a China-based AI lab.
