Why Mistral?

European-built AI models designed for cost-conscious developers who need reliable performance.

🇪🇺

European Data Sovereignty

Mistral is headquartered in Paris and stores data in Europe. Ideal for GDPR-sensitive workloads and EU-based companies that need to comply with strict data residency requirements.

🔓

Open-Source Heritage

Mistral releases open-weight models alongside their API, giving you the flexibility to self-host or switch providers. No vendor lock-in — deploy on your own infrastructure if needed.

💰

Competitive Pricing

Mistral Large undercuts GPT-4o on price while delivering strong benchmark results. Mistral Small is one of the cheapest options available at $0.10/$0.30 per 1M tokens.

Which Mistral Model Should You Use?

Mistral offers two tiers designed for different workloads.

Mistral Large

$2.00 input / $6.00 output per 1M tokens
  • 128K context window
  • Strong reasoning and code generation
  • Complex multi-step tasks
  • Document analysis and summarization
  • Multilingual support
Best for: Production workloads that require high accuracy and long context. Ideal replacement for GPT-4o when cost is a priority.

Mistral Small

$0.10 input / $0.30 output per 1M tokens
  • 32K context window
  • Fast and lightweight
  • Excellent for simple classification tasks
  • Cost-effective at scale
  • Great for chatbots and short responses
Best for: High-volume, low-complexity tasks like classification, sentiment analysis, and simple Q&A. One of the cheapest options on the market.
