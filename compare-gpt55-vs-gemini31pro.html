Budget Chatbot / Customer Support

Code Generation

Pick Google: Gemini 3.1 Pro at $2/$12 is 2.5x cheaper than GPT-5.5 ($5/$30) with comparable coding performance. Both have 1M context.

Long Document Analysis

Processing large documents, legal contracts, or codebases. Context window is critical.

Startup MVP / Side Project

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gemini 3.1 Pro cheaper than GPT-5.5?

Yes. Gemini 3.1 Pro ($2/$12 per 1M tokens) is 2.5x cheaper on input and 2.5x cheaper on output than GPT-5.5 ($5/$30). At the budget tier, Gemini 2.0 Flash ($0.10/$0.40) is 2.5x cheaper than GPT-5 mini ($0.25/$2). Google offers consistently lower prices across all model tiers.

What is the cheapest model between OpenAI and Google?

GPT-oss 20B at $0.08/$0.35 is the cheapest option from either provider — 2x cheaper than GPT-oss 20B ($0.08/$0.35) and 3.3x cheaper than GPT-5 mini ($0.25/$2). Even Gemini 2.0 Flash ($0.10/$0.40) beats all OpenAI budget models on price while offering a 1M context window.

Can Gemini 3.1 Pro replace GPT-5.5 for production workloads?

For many use cases, yes. Gemini 3.1 Pro ($2/$12) matches or exceeds GPT-5 ($1.25/$10) on many benchmarks at a similar price point, and is 2.5x cheaper than GPT-5.5 ($5/$30). Both offer 1M context windows. Choose GPT-5.5 when you need the absolute highest reasoning quality or deep integration with the OpenAI ecosystem. For most production workloads — chat, content generation, data processing — Gemini 3.1 Pro delivers excellent value.

GPT-5 vs Gemini 3.1 Pro: which is better?

GPT-5 ($1.25/$10) and Gemini 3.1 Pro ($2/$12) are similarly priced mid-tier models. GPT-5 is slightly cheaper on input (37% less) while Gemini 3.1 Pro offers a 1M context window vs GPT-5's 272K. For most tasks, both perform comparably. Choose GPT-5 for cost-sensitive workloads; choose Gemini 3.1 Pro when you need the larger context window.

GPT-5.5 vs Gemini 3.1 Pro: which should I choose?

GPT-5.5 ($5/$30) is OpenAI's most capable model with 1M context. Gemini 3.1 Pro ($2/$12) is 2.5x cheaper with the same 1M context. For most tasks, Gemini 3.1 Pro provides excellent value. Choose GPT-5.5 only when you need peak reasoning ability, complex multi-step analysis, or deep OpenAI ecosystem integration. For code generation, summarization, and general tasks, Gemini 3.1 Pro delivers comparable quality at 40% of the cost.
