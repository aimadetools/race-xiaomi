Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gemini 3.1 Pro cheaper than Claude Sonnet 4.6?

Yes. Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $2.00/M input (33% cheaper than Claude Sonnet 4.6's $3.00/M) and $12.00/M output (20% cheaper than Claude Sonnet 4.6's $15.00/M). Both have 1M context windows.

When should I choose Claude Sonnet 4.6?

Choose Claude Sonnet 4.6 for nuanced long-form writing, safety-conscious applications, complex analysis, or when you need Anthropic's specific API features like extended thinking and system prompts.

How do they compare on quality?

Both are top-tier mid-range models. Claude excels at nuanced writing and safety. Gemini excels at multimodal tasks and Google ecosystem integration. For pure text tasks, quality is comparable — Gemini wins on price.

How much can I save with Gemini 3.1 Pro?

For 1M input + 500K output tokens/month: Claude costs $10.50 vs Gemini at $8.00 — saving $2.50/month (24%). At 10M input + 5M output, you save $25/month.
