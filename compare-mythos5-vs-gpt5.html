Frequently Asked Questions

Is GPT-5 cheaper than Claude Mythos 5?

Yes. GPT-5 costs $1.25 input and $10.00 output per 1M tokens, while Claude Mythos 5 costs $10.00 input and $50.00 output. GPT-5 is 87% cheaper on input and 80% cheaper on output. However, Mythos 5 has a larger context window (1M vs 272K tokens).

What's the difference between Claude Mythos 5 and GPT-5?

Claude Mythos 5 is a premium Anthropic model with 1M context and specialized creative reasoning ($10/$50). GPT-5 is OpenAI's flagship model with 272K context ($1.25/$10). GPT-5 is 80% cheaper but has 3.7x less context. Mythos 5 excels at creative and narrative tasks, while GPT-5 is a strong all-rounder.

Is Claude Mythos 5 worth the extra cost over GPT-5?

It depends on your needs. Mythos 5 costs 5-8x more but offers 3.7x more context (1M vs 272K) and excels at creative reasoning and long-form content. If you need to process very large documents or require premium creative output, Mythos 5 may be worth it. For most general tasks, GPT-5 at 80% lower cost is the better value.

Which has a larger context window: Mythos 5 or GPT-5?

Claude Mythos 5 has a 1M token context window, while GPT-5 has 272K tokens. Mythos 5 offers 3.7x more context capacity, making it better for processing very large documents, long conversations, or complex multi-step tasks that require extensive context.

How much can I save using GPT-5 instead of Mythos 5?

At typical usage (10M input + 5M output tokens/month), you'd spend $350/month on Mythos 5 vs $62.50/month on GPT-5 — saving $287.50/month or $3,450/year. That's an 82% cost reduction, though you'll have less context capacity (272K vs 1M).

When should I choose Mythos 5 over GPT-5?

Choose Mythos 5 when you need: 1) Very large context windows (1M vs 272K), 2) Premium creative and narrative reasoning, 3) Processing long documents without chunking, 4) Anthropic's safety standards and quality guarantees. For most other use cases, GPT-5 offers better value at 80% lower cost.
