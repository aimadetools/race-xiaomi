Finance-Specific Considerations

  • Numerical accuracy: Financial calculations require precision. Budget models can handle data extraction and formatting well, but complex numerical reasoning benefits from premium models with stronger analytical capabilities.
  • Regulatory compliance: Financial services have strict data handling requirements (SOC 2, PCI DSS, GDPR). Ensure your AI provider meets your regulatory obligations and consider data residency requirements.
  • Real-time market data: If your AI needs to process real-time market feeds, consider latency requirements. Batch processing for reports and analysis can use cheaper async APIs.
  • Audit trails: Financial applications often require audit logging. Track which model processed each task and maintain version control of AI-generated outputs for compliance purposes.
  • Hybrid approach: Many financial firms use AI for initial draft and human analysts for review. This augments analyst productivity without replacing judgment on investment decisions or risk assessments.
