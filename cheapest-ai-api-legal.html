Legal-Specific Considerations

  • Accuracy matters more than speed: Legal documents require high precision. A missed clause or incorrect extraction can have serious consequences. Consider premium models for critical contracts.
  • Context window is critical: Legal documents are often 50-100+ pages. Models with large context windows (Gemini Flash at 1M, Claude at 200K) handle full contracts without chunking.
  • Input-heavy workload: Legal review is 80-95% input tokens (reading documents) with relatively short outputs (findings, summaries). Input price dominates your cost.
  • Batch processing: Most legal review is batch (review N documents), not real-time. This means you can optimize by processing during off-peak hours and using async APIs.
  • Compliance requirements: Ensure your AI provider meets your jurisdiction's data handling requirements. Some legal work requires on-premise or private cloud deployment.
