HR-Specific Considerations

  • EEOC compliance: AI screening must not discriminate based on protected characteristics. Use structured prompts that evaluate qualifications only. Audit model outputs for bias.
  • Data privacy: Resumes contain PII (names, addresses, employment history). Ensure your provider signs a DPA and offers data processing agreements. Consider anonymizing resumes before sending to the API.
  • Employer brand: Job descriptions represent your company. Premium models produce more engaging, inclusive job posts that attract diverse candidates.
  • Volume patterns: Resume screening spikes during hiring surges. Budget models handle volume cost-effectively; scale to premium only for final-round evaluation.
  • Legal nuance: Employment law varies by jurisdiction. For policy compliance and legal analysis, use premium models with stronger reasoning to catch subtle issues.
