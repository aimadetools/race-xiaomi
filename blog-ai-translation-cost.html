When to Use Each Model

UI strings, internal docs, error messages, product feeds. At $0.0000021/word, you can translate 100M words for $210. Good enough for anything where perfect fluency isn't critical.

Product Localization: GPT-5 mini

App interfaces, marketing copy, product descriptions. At $0.0000113/word, the quality jump from budget models is visible in natural phrasing and idiom handling. Best value for customer-facing content.

Cost Optimization Tips

Three strategies that cut translation costs by 30-60%:

1. Two-tier routing. Use DeepSeek V4 Flash for high-volume, low-stakes content (UI strings, error messages). Reserve GPT-5 mini or GPT-4o for customer-facing marketing copy. This alone cuts costs by 60-80%.

2. Translation memory. Cache previously translated strings. A 30% cache hit rate reduces API costs by 30% with zero quality loss. Essential for products with recurring content (monthly newsletters, quarterly reports).

3. Batch processing. Group similar strings into single requests. Instead of translating 1,000 button labels individually, batch them into one request with clear separators. Fewer API calls = lower overhead.
