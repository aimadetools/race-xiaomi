Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is Mistral Medium 3.5?

Mistral Medium costs $1.50/$7.50 per 1M tokens vs Sonnet 5 at $3/$15 — 50% cheaper on both input and output. At 1M input + 500K output tokens/month, that's $5.25 vs $10.50, saving $5.25/month.

Is Sonnet 5 worth 2x the cost of Mistral Medium?

Sonnet 5 at $3/$15 offers Anthropic's latest model quality with 1M context (8x larger than Mistral's 128K). For tasks requiring massive context windows or Anthropic's specific model strengths, the 2x premium may be justified.

When should I use Mistral Medium over Sonnet 5?

Choose Mistral Medium 3.5 ($1.50/$7.50) for budget-conscious workloads where 128K context is sufficient. Choose Sonnet 5 ($3/$15) for tasks requiring 1M context or when Anthropic's model quality is preferred.
