Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

APIpulse is a static website. We do not collect personal information. All calculations happen in your browser — no data is sent to our servers.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited and time spent on the site. No personal information is collected.

Cookies

GA4 uses cookies to distinguish between unique users. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting the site's functionality.

Pro purchases

If you purchase Pro access, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your payment information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address to send pricing updates. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in each email.
