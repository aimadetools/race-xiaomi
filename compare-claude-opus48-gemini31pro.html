Is Claude Opus 4.8 more expensive than Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Yes. Claude Opus 4.8 costs $5/$25 per 1M tokens. Gemini 3.1 Pro costs $2/$12 per 1M tokens. Gemini is 60% cheaper on input and 52% cheaper on output — a significant difference at scale.

Which has a larger context window?

Both Claude Opus 4.8 and Gemini 3.1 Pro offer 1M token context windows. They're equally capable for processing very long documents, codebases, or extended conversations.

When should I choose Claude Opus 4.8 over Gemini 3.1 Pro?

Choose Claude Opus 4.8 when you need Anthropic's safety-focused approach, nuanced reasoning, or specific capabilities where Claude excels (like long-form writing and complex analysis). Choose Gemini 3.1 Pro when cost is a primary concern and you want 1M context at a mid-tier price point.
