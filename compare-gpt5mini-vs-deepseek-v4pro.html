Frequently Asked Questions

Which is cheaper: GPT-5 mini or DeepSeek V4 Pro?

It depends on your usage. GPT-5 mini is 43% cheaper on input ($0.25/M vs $0.435/M), but DeepSeek V4 Pro is 57% cheaper on output ($0.87/M vs $2.00/M). For output-heavy workloads, DeepSeek wins. For input-heavy, GPT-5 mini wins.

Which has a better context window?

DeepSeek V4 Pro has a 1M token context window — 3.7× larger than GPT-5 mini's 272K. For long documents or extended conversations, DeepSeek handles significantly more context.

When should I pick GPT-5 mini?

Choose GPT-5 mini if you need OpenAI's ecosystem integration, prefer a US-based provider, or have input-heavy workloads (classification, analysis) where output tokens are minimal.

When should I pick DeepSeek V4 Pro?

Choose DeepSeek V4 Pro for output-heavy workloads (content generation, chatbots), when you need a larger context window (1M vs 272K), or when overall cost matters most. DeepSeek typically wins on total cost.
