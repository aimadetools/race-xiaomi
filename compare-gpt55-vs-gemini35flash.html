Frequently Asked Questions

How much cheaper is Gemini 3.5 Flash than GPT-5.5?

Gemini 3.5 Flash is 70% cheaper on input ($1.50 vs $5.00 per 1M tokens) and 70% cheaper on output ($9.00 vs $30.00 per 1M tokens). For a typical workload of 1M input and 500K output tokens per month, Gemini 3.5 Flash costs $6.00 vs GPT-5.5's $20.00 — a savings of $14.00 per month.

Is Gemini 3.5 Flash as good as GPT-5.5?

GPT-5.5 is OpenAI's premium flagship model, designed for maximum reasoning capability. Gemini 3.5 Flash is Google's mid-tier model optimized for speed and cost-efficiency. For most common tasks (chatbots, content generation, data extraction), Gemini 3.5 Flash delivers excellent results at 70% lower cost. GPT-5.5 excels at complex multi-step reasoning and tasks requiring the highest quality output.

When should I choose GPT-5.5 over Gemini 3.5 Flash?

Choose GPT-5.5 when you need: (1) Maximum reasoning quality for complex tasks, (2) The largest context window (1.05M vs 1M tokens), (3) Enterprise-grade reliability from OpenAI's infrastructure. Choose Gemini 3.5 Flash when cost efficiency matters more than marginal quality gains — it's 70% cheaper with excellent performance for most workloads.

Can I mix GPT-5.5 and Gemini 3.5 Flash to optimize costs?

Yes. Route simple, high-volume tasks (chatbots, content generation, data extraction) to Gemini 3.5 Flash at $1.50/$9.00, and reserve GPT-5.5 ($5.00/$30.00) for complex reasoning, analysis, and tasks requiring maximum quality. This tiered strategy can reduce costs by 40-60% while maintaining quality where it matters most.
