Provider Guide for Startups

What each provider offers beyond raw pricing.

Google Gemini

Most generous free tier (15 RPM, 1M tokens/day). Cheapest models overall. Excellent for prototyping and MVP. 1M context windows. Growing ecosystem with Vertex AI for enterprise.

Best for: Bootstrapped MVPs, high-volume budget workloads

DeepSeek

Best price-to-performance ratio. V4 Pro ($0.44/M) rivals GPT-5 quality at 3x lower cost. 1M context windows on all models. Smaller ecosystem but excellent raw value.

Best for: Cost-conscious production workloads, code-heavy apps

OpenAI

Largest ecosystem, best documentation, most third-party integrations. GPT-5 at $1.25/M is competitive. $5 free credits. Function calling and tool use are industry-leading.

Best for: Enterprise features, function calling, ChatGPT ecosystem

Anthropic Claude

Highest quality reasoning (Opus 4.7). Strong safety guarantees. 1M context on premium models. $5 free credits. Premium pricing but worth it for complex reasoning tasks.

Best for: Complex analysis, safety-critical apps, peak reasoning

Mistral

European provider with strong open-source models. Mistral Small at $0.20/M is competitive. Good for EU data compliance needs. $5 free credits.

Best for: EU compliance, open-source preference, balanced cost/quality

Together.ai / Groq

Hosted open-source models (Llama, Mixtral). Fastest inference (Groq LPU). Llama 3.1 8B at $0.10/M. Great for teams wanting open-source flexibility without self-hosting.

Best for: Open-source models, ultra-fast inference, self-hosting transition

Specific recommendations for common startup AI features.

Customer Support Chatbot

Pick: Gemini Flash Lite ($0.075/M) — 33x cheaper than GPT-5. At 1M messages/month: $0.38 vs $12.50.

Code Generation Feature

Complex reasoning, longer outputs. Quality and accuracy matter for production code suggestions.

Pick: DeepSeek V4 Pro ($0.44/M) — 1M context, excellent coding quality, 3x cheaper than GPT-5.

Content Generation / Marketing

Document Analysis / RAG

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest AI API for startups in 2026?

GPT-oss 20B at $0.08/$0.35 is the cheapest AI API. For startups needing stronger reasoning, DeepSeek V4 Pro at $0.44/$0.87 offers the best value — 7x cheaper than GPT-5 ($1.25/$5) and 11x cheaper than Claude Opus 4.7 ($5/$25). At 1M tokens/month, Gemini Flash Lite costs $3.75, DeepSeek V4 Pro costs $13.10, and GPT-5 costs $62.50.

Which AI API has the best free tier for startups?

Google Gemini offers the most generous free tier with 15 requests per minute and 1M tokens per day on Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite. OpenAI provides $5 in free credits for new accounts. Anthropic offers $5 free credits. Together.ai and Groq offer free tiers for open-source models. For bootstrapped startups, Google's free tier alone can handle early-stage prototyping and MVP testing at zero cost.

Should a startup use OpenAI or DeepSeek for their AI API?

For cost-conscious startups, DeepSeek is the clear winner. DeepSeek V4 Pro ($0.44/$0.87 per 1M tokens) is 3x cheaper than GPT-4o ($2.50/$10) and 3x cheaper than GPT-5 ($1.25/$5) while offering 1M context windows. OpenAI wins on ecosystem maturity, documentation, and enterprise features. Choose DeepSeek if cost is primary. Choose OpenAI if you need the ChatGPT ecosystem, function calling maturity, or enterprise compliance.

How much does it cost to add AI to a startup MVP?

A typical startup MVP using AI APIs costs $5-50/month. At 100K requests/month with 1000 input + 500 output tokens each: Gemini Flash Lite costs $0.38/month, DeepSeek V4 Flash costs $1.68/month, GPT-4o mini costs $4.50/month. Even heavy usage (1M requests/month) stays under $50 with budget models. Use our interactive calculator above to estimate your exact costs.

Is DeepSeek reliable enough for production startup use?

Yes. DeepSeek V4 Pro and V4 Flash are production-grade models used by thousands of companies. They offer 99.9%+ uptime, 1M context windows, and competitive quality on coding and reasoning benchmarks. The main trade-off is a smaller ecosystem compared to OpenAI — fewer SDKs, less documentation, and fewer third-party integrations. For most startup use cases (chatbots, content generation, data processing, code assistance), DeepSeek delivers excellent results at a fraction of the cost.
