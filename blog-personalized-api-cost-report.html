Why "Personalized" Matters

Generic pricing tables tell you what models cost per token. They don't tell you what your savings would be because:

  • Your input/output ratio matters. A chatbot (80% output) has a completely different cost profile than a RAG system (80% input). The report uses your actual ratio.
  • Your volume matters. Some providers offer volume discounts. Others don't. The report accounts for your monthly request count.
  • Your use case matters. A summarization task needs different quality than code generation. The report shows alternatives ranked by cost and quality for your use case.
