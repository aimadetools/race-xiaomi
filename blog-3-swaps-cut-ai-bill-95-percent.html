The Smart Play: Route by Complexity

The best teams don't pick one model — they route by task complexity:

  • Complex tasks (analysis, multi-step reasoning, creative) → GPT-5.4 or Claude Sonnet 5
  • Critical tasks (revenue-impacting, safety-critical) → GPT-5.5 or Claude Opus 4.8

This routing approach typically cuts bills by 70-85% while maintaining quality where it matters.
